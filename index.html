You can start monotiring the pump on your mobile device only if the NGP pump that you have is Bluetooth-enabled. On the next step we will tell you how to check this fact.

The system also assumes that your mobile device is connected to the Internet, so that is can send your therapy data to your profile in CareLink.

So you can proceed an get started if you have the BLE-enabled NGP and your mobile device is connected to the Internet.

This is a preparation step, reminding you to find your pump (if it is not nearby yet). The application on your mobile device is not ready yet for pairing with the pump - it will be when you tap "Next"...

While you see the "Pair pump" screen with the instructions on what to do on your pump, the application on your mobile device is already avertising itself for the NGP to find it and perform pairing.

The app will stay in this state for 15 minutes, which gives you plenty of time to perform the listed instructions on your pump.

The rest of the pairing actions have to be performed on the pump, according to the instructions.

Once the pairing starts, you will see an indication of that on your mobile device, and upon success the application will automatically proceed further.

Please also make sure that the screen of your mobile device is on while you perform the pairing from the pump.
